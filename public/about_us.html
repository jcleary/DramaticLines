Dramatic Lines Publishers is a small independent press founded to promote drama for young people. Publications have a wide variety of theatrical applications including use for drama examinations, auditions, festivals and theatre group performances. Many of the original pieces have proved popular winning entries in worldwide drama festival competitions. Schools use the material for assemblies, lessons, classroom drama, and for school performances. Many of our writers are drama and speech examiners and teachers with first hand drama teaching experience.
